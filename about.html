ABOUT

What is Dolfin?

Dolfin is a free casual learning app that allows you to learn thousands of new things while spending almost no time and absolutly no money. Just browse through our thousands of topics, and pick one that seems cool. Read the 1-2 page info section (which should take you like 5 minutes), and you're done for the day! Then, for the next two weeks, we'll email you 4 questions a day about that cool topic you chose. At the end of the two weeks, you'll have learned a new thing, spent no money, and wasted less than 40 minutes of your time.

Should I use Dolfin?

Do you like learning about random shit? Are you too cheap to pay to learn about random shit? Are you too busy/lazy to spend a lot of time learning about random shit? You've come to the right place!
